THE PROBLEM

Hotter temperatures, more intense storms, melting glaciers, sinking cities, and more. This isn't caused with no reason. It’s caused because of climate crises or also known as global warming. Global warming is caused by a large amount of CO2 being released into the Earth every year from coal, oil, and gas. The climate crisis is hitting us faster than ever, but we have the time and resources to help prevent and reduce the problem.
